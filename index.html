About Us

inGenius is an annual 24-hour hackathon organised by PES University, Electronic City Campus.

It is one of the most prestigious technical flagship events in India, with people all around coming together to participate. We've successfully completed 8 iterations and we're back again, ready for action!

Circumstances may have forced us online, but that also came as a blessing in disguise. Because this time, we've upped the stakes like never before, we're going fully global this year!

That's right, inGenius is open to registrations to each and anyone of you that wants to take part, wherever you are. No restrictions, no criteria, and NO FEE.

CODE OF CONDUCT

inGenius 2020 is an annual 24-hour hackathon organised by PES University, Electronic City Campus. We are a platform for students pursuing their professional degree, to apply knowledge gained in the classroom and prototype meaningful products. As you all know by now, taking into consideration the current situation, inGenius has gone completely online for this year's iteration. However, we still aim to provide an equally pleasurable and a comparable experience to that of all our previous iterations of the event and have taken care to ensure the same. Our team is committed to providing a harassment-free experience and it's up to each one of us to respect and co-operate with each other throughout the event. Keeping that in mind, we have come up with a Code of Conduct and we advise everyone involved in the event to follow it without fail.





Who can attend inGenius 2020?

inGenius is open to all college students from any year, branch or degree across the globe

Is there a registration fee?

No, there is no registration fee

What is the venue?

There is no venue. Due to the pandemic we are conducting the event virtually.

What is the theme of inGenius 2020?

It's an open hack and all domains are allowed

What should I submit at the end of the hackathon?

You will have to show a working proof of concept of your idea.

How will you judge?

The panel of judges will consist of industry experts and professionals. They will go through the executable file and will have an interview round with top 10 teams to decide on final evaluations.

We're here to help you however you need! Feel free to drop us an email. You could also reach out to us on Facebook or on Twitter
